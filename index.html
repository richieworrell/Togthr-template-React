My Music

About Me:

I am a solo artist, guitar player, vocalist, drummer, producer, and engineer from the Appalachian Mountains of Southwest Virginia, with 15 years of experience playing and recording music.

As a solo artist I have two studio albums of original music, including my latest album - Indian Summer (2022). I was also a founding member of the folk/bluegrass band Dogwood & Holly. 

In addition to my own music, I've worked as a session player on a number of studio albums spanning a wide range of genres, playing electric guitar, acoustic guitar, singing, engineering, and mixing. Music is my passion, and I'd love to pour that passion into your project.
